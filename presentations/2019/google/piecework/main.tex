\begin{frame}[t]{Piecework}
  Contextualizing the \alert{on--demand work} phenomenon as a whole in some broader context, and figure out where to go from there.

    \only<1>{
    \large{\textbf{What we did}}
    \begin{itemize}
      \item Systematic \alert{historical review} of some 150 publications spanning 200 years
      \item Mapping \alert{themes} in research into gig work to developments in piecework
    \end{itemize}
    }
    \only<2->{
    \large{\textbf{Some takeaways}}
      \begin{itemize}
        \item<0,2,4> We've seen gig work before (e.g. ``\alert{surge pricing}'' and other management techniques)
        \item<0,3,4> We can affect workers' conditions, empowered by \alert{historical theory}, with \alert{digital systems}
      \end{itemize}
    }
\end{frame}
